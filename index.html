Guild Overview

Bricked Up RN is a new laid-back, progression-oriented guild on the Area 52 (Horde).

Our goal is to build a social guild, balanced between PvE and PvP.

Currently progressing through Heroic Nerub-ar Palace (5/8), aiming for a full mythic clear.

About Us

Bricked Up RN is a progression-oriented, laid-back guild on the Horde side of Area 52. Founded during the War Within expansion, we focus on both PvE and PvP content, aiming to create a balanced and social environment where players can enjoy all aspects of the game while pushing towards higher goals.
