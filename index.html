Guild Overview

Founded during the War Within expansion, Bricked Up RN is a progression-oriented, laid-back guild on the Horde side of Area 52.

We focus on both PvE and PvP content, aiming to create a balanced and social environment.

PvE Focus and Progression

Our primary focus is PvE content, including raids and mythic+ dungeons.

Currently progressing through Heroic Nerub-ar Palace (5/8), aiming for a full mythic clear.

PvP Aspirations

While PvE is our core, we are ramping up our PvP presence.

Looking to build a solid PvP team to push Rated Battlegrounds and Arena content.
